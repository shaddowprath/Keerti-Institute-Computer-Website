About Us

Getting a good start in a career is a cherished dream for every career aspirant. However, this is easier said than done especially in today's challenging career space. To achieve this dream basic college education is rarely sufficient, especially in India. Today's highly competitive and demanding employment market is looking for only talented and skilled manpower with enough command on the industry requisites. To make a mark you must have the necessary knowledge, skills, and capability to cater to the specific industry you aspire to enter into and flourish. This is equally true across domains of Technology, Mobility services, Designing, IT, BFSI and so on.
Keerti Institute, an initiative of KEERTI KNOWLEDGE AND SKILLS LTD. is an institution that conducts training programs aimed at helping young career seekers understand the basic operational functioning of the sector and the job role they aspire to take on. Our uniquely designed programs empower young individuals to garner skills and knowledge for the modern global industry. The curriculums of the program conducted at Keerti Institutes are developed by industry experts taking into consideration the current environment and challenges faced by recruiting sectors. Their insight and vision have assisted us to enhance the knowledge and skills of youngsters to take on modern-day corporate challenges.
