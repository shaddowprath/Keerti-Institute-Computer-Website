Institute Policies

Attendance Policy: Students are required to maintain at least 75% attendance in all classes.

Code of Conduct: All students must adhere to the institute's code of conduct, which includes respect for faculty, staff, and fellow students.

Examination Policy: All examinations are to be taken on the scheduled dates. No exceptions will be made unless in the case of a medical emergency with proper documentation.

Fee Policy: Fees must be paid by the due dates. Late payments may incur additional charges.
